Marco is your freight-ops coworker in Slack: ask about live Italian-port freight
traffic, port congestion, vessels and ETAs, and set proactive alerts.

## Getting started

1. Add Marco to your Slack workspace via the **Add to Slack** button.
2. Marco will DM you to say hello and ask which ports/operators to watch.
3. **@mention Marco** in any channel, or DM him, to ask a question.

## Things you can ask

- "Which ports are congested right now?"
- "Where's the MOBY FANTASY?"
- "Watch Genoa and tell me when it clears."
- "List my active alerts" / "stop watching Genoa."

## Common questions

- **Marco didn't reply.** In channels you must @mention him; thread follow-ups
  currently need another @mention.
- **The numbers changed between answers.** The data is a live feed and shifts between
  readings — Marco timestamps figures so you can see when they were taken.
- **"You're not authorized to approve actions."** Only approvers configured for your
  workspace can approve actions; the person who installed Marco is an approver by
  default.

## Removing Marco

Slack → **Manage apps** → Marco → **Remove**. This deletes your workspace's stored
token and configuration. See the [Privacy Policy](./PRIVACY.md).
